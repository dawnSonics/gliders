Gliders

Connect to your DAW:

Gliders is an experimental sequencer that flies. By shooting gliders at an ever morphing tonal landscape, you are able to draw out patterns that are at once unpredictable and melodic.

Gliders works by sending MIDI directly into your DAW via a virtual MIDI bus. You can pipe the MIDI into any synth or sampler.

Open this page on your desktop browser to use Gliders with your DAW.
